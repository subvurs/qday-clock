Mosca's inequality

Mosca's inequality frames the migration question as x + y < z, where:

  • x — how long you need your data to remain confidential (years).
  • y — how long it will take to migrate to PQC (years).
  • z — how long until a CRQC arrives (years).

If x + y >= z, harvest-now-decrypt-later attacks affect data you protect today. The Q-day Clock supplies a reading-shaped view of z; x and y are properties of your environment. This page does not compute a probability or a calendar date.
