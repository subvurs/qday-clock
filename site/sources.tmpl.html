What this page is

Every signal that contributed to the current dashboard reading appears below with its evidence class, axis, normalized value in [0, 1], confidence, and a link back to the underlying article when available. Signal IDs match the contributing_signal_ids in the signed clock_state.json — this is the audit trail.

Evidence-class tags follow the methodology: theory, simulation, hardware, roadmap, policy, survey. Roadmap signals are capped per axis (see methodology); they do not move the clock the same way a measured hardware result does.

Honesty notes

  • Signal lists are not necessarily complete: a signal can be present in the corpus and still fail a gate (e.g., RoadmapWeightCapGate, StaleSignalGate) such that its contribution is capped or decayed. The dashboard records which gates fired in this reading.
  • Reversals are recorded with the same prominence as advances. A signal that previously contributed and was later retracted remains in data/history.jsonl; nothing is rewritten.
  • This reading uses only public evidence. Classified intelligence about quantum-hardware programs is out of scope and not represented here.
